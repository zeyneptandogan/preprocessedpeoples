Movies have long held a mirror to society and offered viewers the opportunity to share different experiences and emotions. While doing this, movies generally focus on emotions such as sadness and joy and see the reflection of society. In this context, it struggles with the problem of gender bias, which is also seen in daily life. So how did this effort manifest itself in the process? Let's generally examine the status of gender representation in the movie industry from many aspects and its impact on the results obtained and its change over time.
